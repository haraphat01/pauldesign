About Us

Where craftsmanship
meets artistry.

Based in Toronto, we provide over 30 years of international experience in high-end painting, decorating and restoration, for residential and commercial retail interiors. We see the job through, completing every detail with the highest level of quality and care.

Our team of highly-trained and experienced professionals provide hands-on experience using the best quality materials for your budget. Artistic knowledge and craftsmanship has been, and will continue to be, the main focus of our career and business.

What We Do

Specialist services for
discerning interiors.

01

Restoration

  • Gum-wood paneling
  • Wood graining — Maple, Oak, Mahogany
  • Gilding of Foils — Silver, Gold, Custom tones
  • Plaster molding restoration
  • Refinishing of walls and drywall repairs
02

Wallpaper Application

  • Grass-cloth, Linen, or Mylar (mainly non-pasted)
  • Wallpaper stripping and plaster skimming

* Walls are skimmed and primed prior to wallpaper application.

03

Murals

  • Cotton duct murals — transferable and easily transported
  • Residential and Commercial usage
  • Murals on tiles and canvas

* Work we produce is high detail, with realistic features.

04

High-End Painting, Plastering & Specialty Finishes

  • High-end latex and oil based finishes
  • Faux-finishes and colour consultation
  • Olde World European style refinishing (latex or oil glaze)
  • Metallic spray — alternative to leaf/foil application
  • Wall and trim painting — airless or manual
  • Over-glazing of painted surfaces
  • Marbling and aging surface techniques
  • Concrete mason and plaster finishes
  • Gilded ceilings — Gold, Silver, or Florentine leaf
  • Marmorino Plaster
  • Venetian polished plaster
